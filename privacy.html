TRACKTION
Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 2026
The Short Version

Your data stays on your device. We don't sell it, share it, or use it for ads. Ever.

What We Collect

When you use Tracktion, you provide information like:

  • Your name and email address
  • Body stats — date of birth, height, weight, body fat percentage
  • Fitness goals and activity level
  • Daily food logs and macro targets
  • Your Anthropic API key (used to power AI features)
Where Your Data Lives

Right now, all your data is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage (localStorage). Nothing is sent to our servers.

In the future, we plan to offer optional cloud sync via Supabase so your data works across multiple devices. This will be opt-in, and you'll be notified before it changes.

AI Features & Anthropic

Tracktion uses the Anthropic AI API to analyze food photos, estimate macros, and power the coaching features. When you use these features:

  • Your food photos and descriptions are sent to Anthropic's servers for analysis
  • Anthropic processes this data according to their own privacy policy
  • You provide your own API key — we never see or store your key on our servers

You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com.

What We Don't Do
  • We do not sell your data to anyone
  • We do not show ads
  • We do not share your data with third parties (except Anthropic for AI features as described above)
  • We do not track you across other websites or apps
Your Rights

You are in full control of your data. At any time you can:

  • Delete all your data from within the app (Settings → Privacy & Data → Clear All Data)
  • Delete your account entirely (Settings → Account → Delete Account)
  • Export your data — coming soon
Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll notify you within the app. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent version.
